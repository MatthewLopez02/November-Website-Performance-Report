Key Insights & Findings

Post-Campaign Traffic Stabilization

After the dramatic traffic drop in early October (from ~19K to ~2K daily sessions), November showed stable organic traffic averaging 2,100 sessions/day. This represents the true organic baseline without paid advertising.

Exceptional Engagement Quality

November's engagement rate reached 96.5% with bounce rates as low as 2.5-4%. This indicates organic traffic is highly qualified and genuinely interested in MyBambu services.

Mobile-First Audience

Mobile devices dominate with 85.2% of all sessions (204.7K). Android leads with 52.0% of users, followed by iOS at 45.3%, reflecting the target demographic.

Organic Social Dominance

Organic Social leads all channels with 78.3K sessions (31.2%), driven primarily by TikTok traffic. Direct traffic follows at 59.9K (23.9%), showing strong brand awareness.

TikTok as Primary Driver

TikTok traffic contributed 48.7K sessions (19.4% of total), making it the single largest identified traffic source. This organic social presence continues to drive awareness without paid spend.

Paid Landing Pages Decline

Paid landing pages saw minimal traffic post-campaign shutdown. The Tarjeta (Card) and Recompensas pages dropped to zero after Oct 7, with only IMT pages showing residual visits.

Email Channel Performance

Email marketing drove 34.2K sessions (13.7%), with SFMC contributing 22.3K and Insider adding 11.3K. Email remains a critical retention channel for engaging existing users.

Strong SEO Foundation

Organic Search contributed 46.9K sessions (18.7%), primarily from Google (45.7K). This organic search presence provides sustainable traffic independent of paid advertising.
